IrisNet Mailing Lists

If you want to follow the IrisNet development, you can subscribe to our mailing lists. You can also browse the hypermail archives of these mailing lists.

Currently the following mailing lists exist:

  • iris-announce: Announcements for new versions and other significant events.
  • irislist: Discussion of IrisNet, help with problems and exchange of ideas.
  • irisdev: Development related discussions of IrisNet.

To subscribe/unsubscribe to any of these mailing lists, send a request mail from your email account to majordomo@iris.wv.cc.cmu.edu. The body of the mail should contain a standard Majordomo command. (Subject of the request mail is ignored). Typical commands include

  • subscribe <list> [<address>] (e.g., subscribe irisnet-list)
  • unsubscribe <list> [<address>] (e.g., subscribe irisnet-list)
  • info <list> (e.g., info irisnet-list)

For more information, send us mail.
